About Rann Neeti

welcome to RANN NEETI

Let me guess you are an awesome sportsperson, won a lot of tournaments, think you are better than everyone? Think Again. IIT Mandi presents to you the 6th Edition of the most power packed, fiery and competitive sports event of the north, Rann-Neeti. Compete to prove that you have what it takes to be called a champion.

RannNeeti not only serves as a competitive platform but also a platform where a person may develop his own skills and grow as a better sportsperson.
